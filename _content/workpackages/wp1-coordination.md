## Overview

Work Package 1 serves as the backbone of the Health-AI project, ensuring all activities are coordinated effectively and objectives are met on time.

### Key Activities

- **Project Management**: Day-to-day coordination of all work packages and partners
- **Communication**: Internal and external stakeholder engagement
- **ELSA Management**: Addressing Ethical, Legal, and Societal Aspects throughout the project
- **IP & Exploitation**: Managing intellectual property and commercialization strategies

### Deliverables

This work package produces regular progress reports, meeting minutes, and ensures compliance with all funding requirements.
