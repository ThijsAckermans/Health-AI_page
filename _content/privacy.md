## Introduction

This privacy statement explains how the Health-AI project collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Data Controller

The data controller for this project is the Health-AI Consortium, coordinated by Medical Data Works B.V.

## What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

- Contact information (name, email address)
- Professional information (organization, role)
- Newsletter subscription preferences

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- Respond to your inquiries
- Send newsletters (if subscribed)
- Improve our website and services

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing
- Data portability

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact: info@health-ai.nl
