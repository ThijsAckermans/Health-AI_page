## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Health-AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website.

## What Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect the following information:

- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited
- **Contact information:** If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name and email address

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain our website
- Analyze website usage to improve our content and services
- Respond to your inquiries

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

- **Legitimate interest:** For website analytics and improvement
- **Consent:** For any optional data collection (e.g., contact forms)

## Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Analytics data is anonymized after 26 months.

## Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Data portability

## Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics. These services may collect information about your use of our website. We ensure that all third-party processors comply with GDPR requirements.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at the address provided on our Contact page.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
