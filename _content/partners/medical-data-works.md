## About Medical Data Works

Medical Data Works B.V. is a leading Dutch company specializing in health data management and distributed infrastructures. As the **project coordinator** of Health-AI, MDW brings extensive expertise in:

- **FAIR Data Principles** - Making health data Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable
- **Software Development** - Building solutions for data analytics in healthcare
- **Distributed Infrastructures** - Enabling secure data sharing across organizations

### Role in Health-AI

As coordinator, Medical Data Works leads **Work Package 1 (Coordination)**, ensuring effective project management, communication, and stakeholder engagement across all consortium partners.
